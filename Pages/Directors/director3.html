Biography

Peter is the Chief Marketing Officer at LuxeAura Gifts and is responsible for developing and implementing innovative marketing strategies that strengthen the company’s brand identity. He manages social media activities together with advertising initiatives and brand collaborations to help LuxeAura attract customers who seek special and high-end gifts. He creates emotional and thoughtful gifts through his marketing efforts which focus on storytelling. LuxeAura has become a well-known and trustworthy lifestyle gifting brand because of his innovative brand development work.
