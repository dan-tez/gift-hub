Biography

James serves as Chief Technology Officer at LuxeAura Gifts where he directs all technology projects and digital transformation initiatives. He creates and operates the company online systems which provide access to the e-commerce platform and digital customer experience solutions. James possesses extensive skills in both web development and information technology. He ensures that LuxeAura's digital platforms provide secure and responsive access while maintaining user-friendly features for customers who want to browse gift collections. Through his efforts he connects modern technological solutions with luxury retail which enables customers to browse through premium gifts and complete their online purchases.
